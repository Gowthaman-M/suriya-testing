About Botathon


A Botathon is a meeting ground for student bot enthusiasts using Automation Anywhere platform. Put your bot-building skills to the test and compete in virtual and in-person bot-building events. Achieve bot greatness! Create bots to solve problems and change the world. Play to win–and to learn from your fellow bot compatriots. - and to learn from your fellow bot compatriots.

Salient features of National Botathon 2020

India’s very first RPA Hackathon at a national level, organized by NASSCOM in association with Automation Anywhere.
India’s first Cloud Native RPA Hackathon for Academia, based on Automation Anywhere Enterprise A2019 Community Edition to bring scale and support social distancing during Covid 19.
Participants to receive academic credits provided by AICTE to make National Botathon 2020 as part of regular & mainstream academics.
Industry oriented use cases to be provided to the students to make the utilization of Robotic Process Automation real.
Webinar sessions to be conducted by Automation Anywhere SMEs to bring clarity on Industry use of RPA – focus on blended learning.
All the Bots submitted by the students will be evaluated by an Evaluator Bot, no dependency on human evaluators/SMEs – saving time, resource and scaling up the possibilities.
Access to A People Community – to share information, follow topics & find solution run by socially connected professionals across the globe.
Bot Aspirant Badge A2019 & Co-branded Certificates issued by NASSCOM on completion.
